What We Offer

Our Comprehensive RTO Services

At Shreenath Auto Advisers, we offer a complete range of RTO services designed to cover all your needs. Whether you are a new vehicle owner or need to manage documentation for an existing vehicle, our team is equipped to handle every requirement with expertise. Below is a detailed list of what we offer.

New Vehicle Registration

Start your journey on the right foot. We provide complete assistance with the registration process for your new car or bike, ensuring all documents are correctly filed for a swift and error-free process.

Transfer of Ownership

Buying or selling a used vehicle? We manage the seamless transfer of vehicle ownership, handling all necessary paperwork and RTO procedures to make the transition official and legally compliant.

Driving License Services

Whether you're getting your first learner's license, upgrading to a permanent license, or renewing an existing one, we provide expert guidance to make the process straightforward and successful.

Vehicle Fitness Certificate

Ensure your vehicle is always compliant and road-worthy. We assist in obtaining or renewing your vehicle's fitness certificate, managing the inspection process and documentation on your behalf.

No Objection Certificate (NOC)

Moving to another state? We help you obtain an NOC for transferring your vehicle, navigating all inter-state formalities so you can re-register your vehicle at your new location with ease.

Hypothecation Removal

Once you've paid off your vehicle loan, it's crucial to remove the bank's lien from your RC. We offer complete assistance with removing the hypothecation from your RC book after loan closure.

Duplicate RC/License

Lost or damaged your documents? Don't worry. We help with the official process of obtaining a duplicate Registration Certificate or Driving License in a timely manner.

Address Change in RC/DL

If you've moved, your documents need to reflect your new address. We provide quick and efficient assistance with updating your address on both your RC book and driving license.

All RTO Consulting

Have a unique or complex RTO issue? For any other RTO-related work, feel free to contact us. We provide expert advice and custom solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Vehicle Insurance Services

Stay protected on the road. We assist with new insurance policies and timely renewals, helping you find the best coverage from reputable providers at competitive rates.

Commercial Vehicle Permits

We handle the application and renewal of various commercial permits, including goods carriers, tourist vehicles, and more, ensuring your business operations remain compliant.

Fancy Number Registration

Want a special registration number for your vehicle? We guide you through the process of applying for and acquiring a choice or fancy number through the official RTO channels.
